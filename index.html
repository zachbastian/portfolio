Why I Started Coding

I chose to participate in Epicodus because I want to start a fun, lucrative career. Although I have used computers since an early age, I never got too involved with the technical aspects.

I really like coding because it allows me to be creative, solve problems, and think on my feet. I enjoy the opportunity it gives me to grow cognitively. The challenge is great at certain times, and those challenges help me improve my abilities.
