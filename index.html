About Me

Since an early age, I have been a global citizen. I was born in the States and moved to Europe when I was six years old - to Sweden, and then to Germany. This has given me a unique perspective on the world that I have carried with me throughout my life.

I love to learn and am always open to seeing other people's perspectives. That's what makes life fun! I enjoy communicating with just about anyone and am always looking to make new friends.

Why I Started Coding

I chose to participate in Epicodus because I want to start a fun, lucrative career. Although I have used computers since an early age, I never got too involved with the technical aspects.

I really like coding because it allows me to be creative, solve problems, and think on my feet. I enjoy the opportunity it gives me to grow cognitively.
